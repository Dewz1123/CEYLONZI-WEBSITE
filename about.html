Our Story

Bringing the authentic heritage of Sri Lanka to the global stage.

Rooted in the Pearl of the Indian Ocean

CEYLONZI was born out of a profound love for the rich, fertile lands of Sri Lanka. Known historically as Ceylon, this island nation has been revered for centuries as the world's premier source of exquisite spices and world-class tea.

Our journey started with a simple vision: to bridge the gap between local, hardworking farmers in Sri Lanka and connoisseurs of fine tastes across the globe. We bypass middlemen to ensure that the farmers receive fair compensation while our customers receive the freshest, most authentic products.

"We don't just sell spices and tea; we share a piece of our culture, our heritage, and our passion."

Our Core Values

Premium Quality

We hand-select only the highest grade products. From ALBA grade cinnamon to single-estate silver tips tea, quality is our foremost promise.

100% Natural

No artificial flavors, colors, or preservatives. Our products are organic, pure, and exactly as nature intended them to be.

Ethical Sourcing

We prioritize sustainable farming practices and fair trade principles to uplift the local farming communities in Sri Lanka.
